# Accommodation watch sites

Use this checklist when researching accommodation affordability for destination ecosystems.

## Price-tracking and mainstream search

- Google Hotels price tracking
- Booking.com saved searches / watchlists
- Hotels.com / Expedia
- Kayak hotel search

## Discount-hunting and comparison engines

- HotelsCombined
- Trivago
- EuroCheapo

These sites can compile prices from travel agencies, consolidators, and hotel websites. EuroCheapo is especially relevant for budget hotels.

## Flexible-date and deep-discount options

- Priceline
- GetaRoom

These may be useful when travel dates are flexible and the goal is to find deep accommodation discounts.

## Package checks

Also check air-plus-hotel packages on:

- Kayak
- Expedia
- Similar package providers

Packages may sometimes beat booking flights and accommodation separately, especially for short breaks. However, savings may come with weaker customer service, less flexibility, or more complicated support if something goes wrong.

## Working principle

For this project, accommodation should be tracked through affordability thresholds rather than generic “sales”. The useful question is:

> Is there good-enough accommodation near the anchor place below the target nightly price?

Example fields to add later:

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `accommodation_target_nightly_gbp` | Good-value nightly price threshold for 2 people. |
| `accommodation_watch_sites` | Sites to check for this destination. |
| `sample_good_value_area` | Area/neighbourhood to search first. |
| `accommodation_notes` | Caveats, seasonality, or booking advice. |
